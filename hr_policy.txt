Leave Policy:
Employees are entitled to 18 days of paid leave annually. Unused leaves can be carried forward for up to 2 years.

Work Hours:
The standard work hours are from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday. Employees should take a lunch break of 1 hour.

Dress Code:
Employees are expected to wear business casuals on weekdays. Fridays are dress-down days.

Remote Work:
Employees may work remotely up to 2 days a week with prior manager approval.

Code of Conduct:
Employees must behave respectfully with colleagues and follow company ethics policies strictly.
